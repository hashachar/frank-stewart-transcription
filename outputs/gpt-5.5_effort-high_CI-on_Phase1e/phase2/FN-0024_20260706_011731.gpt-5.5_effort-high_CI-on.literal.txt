Jirā̧ydih - it's not clear whether Ibin Rāḍiy would agree to act as such,
and in any case he was absent.

**Ḥagg al-maq‘ad: ‘Id Salāmih vs. Swēlim/‘Awwād (both Ġraygānis)** : 98

    The following case reported by Sālim ‘Tayyig 24 Jan 79, when I got
him and SI talking about the subject of ḥagg al-bakārij. The case took
place around Oct. 1978.
    Some Ġraygānis and others were in the home of ‘Id Salāmih
(the H of the poor woman, 5/299) in al-‘Arīś. An argument developed
between Swēlim ‘Awwād (Ġrayg.) and Salāmih Frayj (Kr.). Swēlim is
Salāmih's driver, and the argument was about his wages. In the course
of it Swēlim threatened Salāmih with a metal tray (ṣīniyyih). Salāmih
gawwam ‘Id.
    It was agreed that Sālim Ibin ‘Tayyig, who was present, should act
as judge, and xwānih [?]lsānih. The case was settled on the spot.
    The first issue was Swēlim's having threatened
Salāmih. ‘Id didt the talking for Salāmih (like SH in ḥagg al-bēt, but
contra what SI says re Jim‘ih, i.e. SI would have represented himself
against śēx ‘Iliy). Sālim's judgement was ṛiḅā‘ and ṛiḅā‘iyyih for
raf‘it as-ṣīniyyih w-tanzīlha. Query: all fāt?
    The second issue was ‘Id's having been activated. Sālim awarded
(a) ḥagg al-bakārij of ṛiḅā‘ and ṛiḅā‘iyyih + the ṣīniyyih
    b-ṛiḅā‘ + 1 pound for each of four finājīl.
(b) manśad of 4 rib‘ān + 3 kilmāt bayāḍ.
