24 Nov 78. Field notes 15


    There has been talk of SH's sister Ḥimdiyyih coming to live with him.
Her small stock all come from her father. If she simply left her H, she cd
in principle take all her stock with her; if she were divorced,
the 2/3 rule would apply. In fact SH says that he doesn't want any more
small stock, and that if she leaves her husband, he should keep them till
he dies (after which SH would insist on their being sold if the question
arose).
    If a woman dies childless, her agnates get all her small stock.
    SH says that all SI's mother's small stock come from a single goat
given her by SI.

**śā‘‘** ( 12)
    Salāmih śā‘‘ al-xabar layyī, S. warned me of some imminent danger.
**śā‘ih** is roughly rumour, news of dubious or uncertain authenticity
**śā‘‘ih** is certain news.
    The statement on t12 needs modification: the speaker may indeed be
the person beaten (or whatever).

**The killing of Sālim b. Sabbāḥ ar-Rṃāg** 5/371
    Mḥi ar-Rṃāg claimed (a) that the killing was a put-up job: he said that
Sallām had induced his wife to complain because he wanted an excuse to beat
or kill Sālim. SH says that in fact there was no previous enmity between
the parties. (b) that Sālim was killed in his sleep.
    The passage on p. 5/372 should read: huṃṃah widdhum fī śakwit al-
ṃaṛah yitba‘ūnī walla yitba‘uw al-Gṣayyir? (or yitba‘awnī/yitba‘aw). The
sense is: am I to pay the manśad (for ibn Sabbāḥ's alleged deed), or
is Sallām to pay the manśad for having put his wife up to it? The verb
is tibi‘, yatba‘, to walk behind someone.

**Xamsih**
    Cf. 5/368. The agnatic descendant of 2a in generation 5 must be male.
SH confirms that it doesn't matter if dead (say, if a boy who died in
infancy).

**Friday 24 Nov. 1978**
    Up at 6.00, into Thamad at 7.00 and work in clinic. Ibrahim ‘Abd al-
‘Aṭiy arrived in his lorry, and with him śēx Farrāj. SI turned up in
Volvo. He had finally not been included in the delegation to Egypt. He had
an option to buy the Volvo for about IL 45,000, but SH didn't agree, so he
takes it back to al-‘ArīḌ̄ in a week and pays IL 2000. In the evening SH
and Ḥsēn went to Ṭarfa, I stayed in Thamad.
